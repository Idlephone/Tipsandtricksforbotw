  • If you shoot a spicy pepper or sunshroom with a fire arrow, it will create an updraft. This can be combined with Revali's Gale or other peppers and shrooms to go even higher.

  • Holding "ZL" while swimming will result in a different swim animation.

  • On Death Mountain, spin attacking with a heavy weapon resets you burn timer.

  • If you keep beating the Champion's Ballad Blights, you'll continue to get unique dialogue for each Champion.

  • Campfires are heavy.

  • Entering Bullet Time will slow the animations for the Thunderstorm Rod and the Ancient Bladesaw in your inventory.

  • You can map pin a flying arrow.

  • There is a special animation for using fairies as fuel for the Master Cycle.

  • If you paraglide onto mud, you place a cryonis block beneath your feet quickly to easily cross mud puddles.

  • The books hidden around Hyrule translate to,
    "April First Today was a dry hot and sunny day I went to school This diary is very important document.
    April Second Yesterday was fools dau Everything that I wrote was false That is all See you Tomorrow."

  • The wheels of the Master Cycle can transport fire from a fire arrow when it's teleported.

  • Round bombs are thrown slightly farther than Square bombs.

  • Link swims faster without clothes on.

  • A Chuchu's weakspot is its eyeballs.

  • While upgrading your sheikah slate, there is a message above on the guidance stone that translates to, “Master using it and you can have this.”
    Which is a reference to the original Legend of Zelda.

  • You need four campfires to create an updraft, but only three to keep the updraft.

  • The gates you walk through at Rito Village denote what floor you're on by the number of feathers on it.

  • The center of buoyancy for every pot is different. Goron pots turn straight up and down.
    normal and Rito pots tilt sideways. Zora pots flip comepletely up and down. Different Sheikah pots turn all three ways.

  • The final memory cutscene is different depending on whether you took a picture of the photo at Impa's house.

  • Rainbows can occur at night.

  • The unlit torches and candles in Hyrule Castle can be lit.

  • You can use magnesis on the bell in Tarrey Town to make a ringing noise.

  • You can break wooden chests to save time. Metal chests are indestructible except for the cannons on Death Mountain.

  • Windmill turn speeds actually depend on the wind.

  • The map changes behind your back while knocking down the Bridge of Eldin and building Tarrey Town.

  • In the Korok forest, Hestu is standing on a maraca island.

  • The hyrule castle library is categorized.

  • Above every armor shop, there is a banner that show the local clothing style.

  • There is a special animation for holding a weapon lit with the blue flame while crouching.

  • For every lock inside the Final Trial, there is a Sheikah letter that denotes which room it is unlocked by.
